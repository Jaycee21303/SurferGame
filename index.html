Cube Run: Trenchfire

Fly a narrow exhaust trench like classic cubefield, but with drift-heavy handling and endless bolts. Thread gaps, blast cubes and hostile craft, and push distance through the trench.

Flight

  • Move: WASD / Arrow Keys
  • Fire: Hold Space / K
  • Pause: P

Objective

Dodge or destroy cube walls and hostile sentries. Maintain your multiplier by passing or blasting without crashing.
