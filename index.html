Neon Hell: Corridor

A browser-ready, Tron-styled corridor shooter. Strafe a glowing trench in first-person, snap headshots downrange, and fight off endless hellspawn.

Core

  • Fire: Hold Space or Left Click

Loop

Waves intensify as distance climbs. Aim with the mouse, strafe through neon debris, and keep armor up by avoiding hits.
