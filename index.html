Trenchfire: Starfall Run

Throttle through a narrow exhaust trench. Drift like our asteroid protoype, spray endless laser bolts, and break the enemy screen before the core vent seals.

Flight Controls

  • Move: WASD / Arrow Keys
  • Fire: Hold Space / K
  • Pause: P

Objective

Stay centered in the trench, dodge mines, shred interceptors and wall sentries, and hammer the core vent before it cycles.
